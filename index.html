How we work with you

Planned around your life, not a template

In Uganda, fewer than one in a hundred people are protected when something goes wrong — among the lowest rates on the continent (Insurance Regulatory Authority, 2025). Impakt Wealth Partners exists to change that — one person, one plan at a time. The FWY 360 approach begins where you are and builds outward, in plain language you actually own.

For women

Too often money decisions get made around women instead of with them. We plan alongside you — clear choices, no jargon, a plan you understand and hold yourself.

For families

One illness or accident shouldn't undo years of work. We put protection and a savings plan in place so your family stays steady when life doesn't.

For young people

Starting early is the biggest advantage you have. We help you build good habits and your first real plan — in language that respects your time.

And the tide is turning: in 2025 more Ugandans chose life cover than ever, with those premiums up 39% in a single year, and insurers paid nearly one trillion shillings back to families and businesses (IRA, 2025). You would be in good, growing company.
